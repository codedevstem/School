Poems of the day.

The suns morning

The day is ready to make it rise,
it roasts, it bakes, it fries.
Then it's good to take a bath
with sea, salt and sand apart

The waves carry tides that the world is greedy.
When work and duty must feel so needy.
Then you dream about the breakers
and feel the crash

And when the day is ending the sun is heavy.

So the moon takes over ever so steady.
