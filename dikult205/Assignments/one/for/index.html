Our Cause

The uses of cannabis is uncounted and in those uncounted uses there might just be a cure for your illness.

We in Extra Chance are fighting for your right to use every weapon to combat you ailment.

Research shows and doctors agree that cannabis is effective to increase appetite and weight gain for patients with many illnesses like HIV/Aids, cancer treatment and more.

We belive that with your help we can find other medical uses for the drug.

Medical cannabis is now legal in 29 states in the US, but we need your help to push for federal law to acknowledge these uses.

New research tells us that cannabis has been acknowledged to be effective to treat epilepsy, MS, cronic pain and ADHD.

But most of all we want to help people like Amanda to battle their illness and give them an Extra Chance.
