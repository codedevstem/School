Poems of a man

The golden rise

The day is ready to make it rise.
It roasts, it bakes, it fries.
Then it's good to take a swim,
in the ocean with joy to the brim.

The waves of today

The waves carry tides that the world is greedy.
When work and duty must feel so needy.
As the clocks tick to a quarter to four
you feel like you need a whole lot more.

Ending of the light

The day is ending, it's getting later
It's time to change the day to greater
Where better to be, yes be so free
than where your hearth wants to be.

The pale sun rises

The day has ended and the night takes hold.
You dream sweet dreams of the beaches gold.
And you know that tomorrow it will begin anew.
To make you rise and make dreams true.
